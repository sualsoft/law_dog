10x10 Duplex Kennel

The Law Dog Kastle provides the safety and comforts of home no matter where the kennel is placed. A standard 10x10 kennel weighs in at approximately 3000 pounds. We can ship anywhere in the Continental United States either in kit form or fully assembled. Once a Law Dog Kastle is assembled, it can be moved at anytime with a tilt trailer or roll-back truck.

The Best housing for your K9 Unit

Our 10x10 duplex unit is the perfect private housing a team of two K9 officers. It features two private 42" x 42" interior cages. They also include comfort features such as windows for fresh air, large outdoor area with removable stainless steel feeder bowls.
