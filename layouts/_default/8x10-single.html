8x10 Single Kennel

The Law Dog Kastle provides the safety and comforts of home no matter where the kennel is placed. Once a Law Dog Kastle is assembled, it can be moved at anytime with a tilt trailer or roll-back truck.

built to last

Law Dog Kastle has been the #1 kennel provider since 1998 to law enforcement agencies across the United States. We can ship anywhere in the Continental United States either in kit form or fully assembled. Once a Law Dog Kastle is assembled it can be moved at anytime with a tilt trailer or roll-back truck.

No concrete or gravel pad needed. The kennel comes with a poly-lumber decking floor on the exterior run (lifetime warranty). The interior decking is sprayed with an epoxy sealant for easy sprayout and more durablity.

You can be rest assured that this kennel with the proper maintenance will last many years to come.
